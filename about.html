Our Story

At Mazza Engineering, versatility is at the core of everything we do. As experienced jobbing subcontractors, we specialize in short production runs—many of which are unique, one-off components. Frequent changeovers and new setups are not just part of the job—they're challenges we embrace every day with skill and precision.


Our ability to deliver consistent quality under demanding conditions stems from a team that values craftsmanship, efficiency, and innovation. Each setup is handled with the utmost attention to detail, ensuring that every client receives a solution tailored to their exact needs.


We pride ourselves on smart, efficient administration powered by advanced computer systems. These tools enable us to streamline our workflow, improve turnaround times, and maintain the highest standards in customer service and machining quality.


Mazza Engineering is also proud to be a Level 4 B-BBEE compliant company, demonstrating our commitment to inclusive growth and transformation in South Africa's engineering sector.
